# Shopify App Store Power BI Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Shopify App Store data using Power BI. The report explores app categories, review trends, average ratings, developer replies, free plan availability, and monthly pricing patterns.

The dashboard includes four main pages:

- Overview
- App Categories
- Reviews Analysis
- Pricing Analysis

## Key Insight

The Shopify App Store shows strong user engagement, with approximately 8,000 reviews across 500 apps. Most reviews are positive, with an average rating of about 4.19. Ratings of 4 and 5 represent the majority of user reviews, suggesting that many apps are generally well received by users.

The number of reviews has grown significantly over time, especially in recent years. This suggests increasing marketplace activity and stronger user participation.

Developer replies are relatively limited. Only about 24.8% of reviews receive a developer response, meaning most reviews do not get a visible response from the app developer.

Category analysis shows that some categories, such as SEO, Reviews and Ratings, Sales and Conversion, and Orders and Shipping, receive higher review activity than others. Pricing also varies across categories, with some categories having higher average monthly prices.

## Business Impact

These insights matter because reviews and ratings influence app visibility, user trust, and conversion in the Shopify App Store. Categories with high review activity may represent stronger customer demand and higher competition.

The low developer reply rate may represent an opportunity for app developers to improve customer support and trust. Responding to reviews can help developers address user concerns, show active maintenance, and improve the perceived quality of their apps.

Pricing insights can help Shopify stakeholders and app developers understand how pricing differs by category. This can support better positioning, pricing strategies, and marketplace analysis.

## Recommendation

Shopify stakeholders should encourage developers to respond to more customer reviews, especially in high-volume categories. Improving developer engagement may help increase user trust and app quality perception.

Developers should monitor review trends and focus on maintaining strong ratings, especially in competitive categories such as SEO, Reviews and Ratings, Sales and Conversion, and Orders and Shipping.

For pricing strategy, developers should compare their monthly prices against category averages to ensure their apps are positioned competitively. Shopify could also use these insights to identify categories with strong demand and support developers with better marketplace guidance.
